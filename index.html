Cricket

Cricket is based on runs and wickets. There is a red ball for a test match and white for a one day match

Golf

There are several types of games in golf Stroke(goes by how many shots you had), Stableford(points based), Par(plus, minus, circle and squares)

Rugby League

In a Rugby League game, rules include: 6 tackles then handover, A try is worth 4 points, A conversion (goal kick after try) is worth 2 points, Field goal is worth 1 point
